- Raw Audio vs. Spectrogram Augmentation

    - Raw Audio
        - Time shifting
        - Time streching
        - Pitch scaling
        - Noise addition
        - Impulse response addition
        - Low/High/Bandpass filters
        - Polarity inversions
        - Random gain.....

    - Spectrogram
        - Time masking (y-achse)
        - Frequency masking (x-achse)
        - Time streching
        - Pitch scaling

- Augmentation Libraryies

    - Librosa (for minimal data augmentation needs)
        - Good Audio Processing library
        - Open source
        - Time strech / pitch shifting
        - Offline Augmentation
        
    - audiomentations (for complex augmentation needs) https://github.com/iver56/audiomentations
        - Open source
        - Loads of transformations (raw audio & spectrograms)
        - Supports augmentation chains (compositions)
        - Online & offline augmentation
        - Integrates with Keras / PyTorch
        - CPU only
